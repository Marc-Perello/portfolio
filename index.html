Some lines about me

I’m a UX / UI Designer currently working at Lola-MullenLowe, and living in Barcelona.
I started years ago as a graphic designer and a photographer for print media. With the adveniment of the digital media I focused my energy on the design of the new digital products.
Nowadays I create astethic and intuitive user interfaces. The making of flows, wireframes, visual designs, interactions and documentation are part of my everyday.
